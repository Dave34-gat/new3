About Us

Eco Grocery Store is a sustainable retail initiative based in Ethiopia, committed to offering fresh, locally-sourced, and environmentally friendly products. Established in 2020, our mission is to provide healthy food choices while minimizing environmental impact through eco-conscious practices and partnerships with local farmers and suppliers.
